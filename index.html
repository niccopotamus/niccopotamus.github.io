Is Architects' New Album, Holy Hell, their best album yet?

Earlier this week, UK metalcore band, Architects released their new album, Holy Hell, and holy hell is what you will be saying when you listen to this album.

Sam's lyrics and vocals have become so much stronger and much more defined. The harmonnies and melodies are some of their strongest yet, and I can't get enough of this album.
